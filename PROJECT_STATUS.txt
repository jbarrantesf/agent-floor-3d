🔄 BIDIRECTIONAL HERMES ↔ ORBIT INTEGRATION

  Hermes (Manager)
    ├─ delegateTask()          [INSERT INTO tasks]
    ├─ subscribeToUpdates()    [LISTEN task_events]
    └─ getAgentStatus()        [SELECT capacity]
            ↓
          Supabase Realtime
            ↓
  ORBIT (Queue)
    ├─ pollForTasks()          [SELECT QUEUED]
    ├─ executeTask()           [File/SQL/Shell/Webhook]
    └─ reportProgress()        [UPDATE tasks, INSERT events]
            ↓
          Supabase Realtime
            ↓
  Hermes (Results)
    ├─ subscribeToCompletion() [Receives COMPLETED]
    ├─ getTaskHistory()        [Analytics]
    └─ printStats()            [Dashboard]
